    A climb against all odds

Dreams Do Come True.

I was going to Arizona State when my dad passed away. I had just paid for the semester, $9,000.00 and decided to drop out and go to New York to follow my dreams of being a stylist. I lied to people saying that I had clients, but now I actually have 42. This has been pure hustle, but I promised my dad I would graduate from college. I missed the deadline for applications, but somehow, they helped me out. I got myself in the door and now I am already finishing my second semester with a 3.64 GPA.

A Tragic Climb.

Climbing has always been my passion. After I had successfully ascended and descended Mt. Everest, I heard of a group of people that had gone up after me and got stuck in a storm. I decided to climb part of the way back up the mountain to save whoever I could find. Though eight people died on Mr. Everest that night, I managed to find and save three others.
